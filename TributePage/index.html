T1 Faker

T1 Faker, the legendary League of Legends player.

T1 Faker, whose real name is Sang-hyeok Lee, is a professional League of Legends player from South Korea. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the game.

Faker has achieved numerous championships with T1 (formerly SK Telecom T1) and is known for his exceptional skill, strategic intelligence, and game-changing performances.
